Free vs Full Version

The full version is not just more sounds. It unlocks the real use of your personal BrainRhythm ID.

The free version lets you experience the idea. The full version lets you use your rhythm profile across the full system of personalized states.

The main difference

You are unlocking the use of your own BrainRhythm ID.

After the scan, BrainRhythm ID creates a personal rhythm profile. That profile is the foundation for personalized rhythm sessions.

The full version gives access to the broader use of that profile: situational rhythms, deeper states, Delta, REM-like mode and repeated use across real-life moments.

Free Version

Experience the concept

The free version lets you try BrainRhythm ID and understand how the personal rhythm system works.

  • Discover the idea of a personal rhythm profile.
  • Open the app and explore the rhythm system.
  • Use one free playable choice to experience personalized rhythm use.
  • Understand how BrainRhythm ID connects scan results with real-life states.
Full Version

Use your full BrainRhythm ID

The full version unlocks the complete use of your personal rhythm profile across the app.

  • Use all situational rhythms connected with your BrainRhythm ID.
  • Access focus, calm, recovery, emotional balance, sleep and performance sessions.
  • Use Delta and REM-like modes for deeper rest and inner processing.
  • Return to your personal rhythm system whenever you need a specific state.
Why full access matters

Your rhythm profile becomes useful through repeated use.

A personal rhythm profile is most useful when you can apply it to different real-life situations. Focus is not the same as sleep preparation. Stress reset is not the same as creative flow. Low inner energy is not the same as nervous tension.

The full version allows the app to use your BrainRhythm ID across these different rhythm directions, instead of limiting you to one small part of the system.

Simple explanation

Free lets you test it. Full lets you live with it.

The free version is a first experience. The full version is the practical system.

With full access, your BrainRhythm ID becomes a personal tool you can return to when you want calm, clarity, focus, recovery, sleep preparation, emotional balance, creative flow, confidence or deeper inner processing.
