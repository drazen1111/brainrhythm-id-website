Your personal rhythm profile

What is your BrainRhythm ID?

Your BrainRhythm ID is your personal brain rhythm profile — the foundation for creating personalized rhythms for real-life mental states.

It is not just a number, a sound, or the end of a scan. It is the beginning of personalized rhythm use.

What you received

Your scan result becomes a working profile.

You received your personal BrainRhythm ID — a profile that shows how your brain responds to different rhythmic states.

This profile becomes the foundation for creating personalized rhythms that can be used to intentionally guide mental states: from alpha calm, theta inner processing and delta rest, to beta focus, mental activation and preparation for real-life tasks.

In other words, the app does not give every user the same rhythm. It uses your personal rhythm profile to create rhythm sessions that are connected with the way your brain enters different states.

Why it matters

Your ID is not the end of the scan.

It identifies your rhythm response

Different people do not respond to rhythm in the same way. BrainRhythm ID is built around the idea that your useful rhythm pattern should come from your own scan response, not from a generic preset.

It becomes the base for real-life use

Your ID can be used as the basis for situational rhythms: focus, stress reset, emotional balance, low inner energy, sports preparation, sleep preparation, creative flow and more.

How it works

From scan to personalized rhythm use

1. You scan your response

You listen to different rhythm patterns and choose the ones that feel most suitable for specific states.

2. The app builds your ID

Your choices create a personal brain rhythm profile connected with alpha, beta, theta, delta and REM-like states.

3. Your ID creates rhythms

The app uses your profile to create personalized rhythms for real-life situations where you need a specific mental state.

The key idea

You are not just listening. You are directing.

BrainRhythm ID is a tool for intentionally guiding your mental states through personalized brain rhythms.

Your rhythm profile can be used when you want to move from tension into calm, from mental fog into focus, from low inner energy into more stability, from overactivation into control, or from exhaustion into deeper recovery.
