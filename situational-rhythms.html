Situational Rhythms

Situational Rhythms use your personal BrainRhythm ID to create rhythm sessions for real moments in everyday life.

They are not random presets. They are personalized rhythm directions for the state you want to enter before, during or after a specific situation.

What they do

Your ID becomes practical.

After the scan, your BrainRhythm ID becomes the base for situational rhythm sessions. These sessions are designed for specific states: focus, calm, confidence, recovery, emotional balance, sleep preparation, creative flow, physical preparation and more.

The purpose is simple: when you know the state you need, the app can use your personal rhythm profile to guide you toward that direction.

Deep Recovery Support

A rhythm for directed inner recovery work.

Deep Recovery Support is not just simple relaxation. It is intended for a meditative state where you lie down, calm the system and use directed thoughts to support an inner recovery process.

This can be useful during difficult states, deep exhaustion, long-term burden or moments when you want to turn attention toward the body, renewal, regeneration and return of inner strength.

Romantic / Intimacy

A rhythm for relaxed confidence in closeness.

Romantic / Intimacy is designed for moments before a romantic meeting, a date, meeting a new girl, or any situation where you want to feel more relaxed, confident and natural in closeness.

The goal is not artificial excitement. The goal is a softer, more natural state with less tension and more ease in connection.
