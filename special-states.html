Universal rhythm states

Delta and REM-like Modes

Delta and REM-like modes are special rhythm states designed for deep rest, inner processing, imagery and directed mental work.

Unlike situational rhythms, these modes are universal states. They are not tied to one single everyday situation, but to deeper forms of rest and inner processing.

Special states

Two modes for deeper rhythm work

Most BrainRhythm ID sessions are connected with real-life situations: focus, stress reset, motivation, sleep preparation, emotional balance and similar states.

Delta and REM-like modes are different. They represent broader rhythm directions that can be used for deep rest, inner imagery, symbolic thinking, emotional processing and directed inner work.

Delta Mode

Deep rest and regenerative slowing down

Delta Mode is designed for a calm, slow and deeply settling state. It is connected with rest, sleep preparation, reduced mental activity and a feeling of inner slowing down.

Use it when you want to step away from mental pressure, calm the system, prepare for deeper rest or create a quiet space for recovery-oriented attention.

Deep rest Sleep preparation Slowing down Recovery state
REM-like Mode

Inner processing, imagery and subconscious content

REM-like Mode is designed for directed thoughts, imagery, symbolic thinking, emotional processing and deeper contact with subconscious content.

Use it when you want to stimulate inner insight, creative images, emotional processing, symbolic thinking or deeper contact with your own subconscious material.

Imagery Inner insight Creative processing Symbolic thinking
Important warning

REM-like mode can be activating.

For some users, REM-like rhythm work can increase mental activity, images, thoughts or inner wakefulness. Because of that, it is not recommended immediately before sleep if it disturbs your ability to fall asleep.

If REM-like mode feels activating, use it earlier in the day or at a time when you want inner processing, creativity, insight or mental imagery rather than sleep.

How to choose

Delta is for settling. REM-like is for processing.

Choose Delta Mode when your goal is to slow down, rest, settle the system or prepare for sleep.

Choose REM-like Mode when your goal is inner imagery, creative processing, directed thoughts, symbolic reflection or deeper contact with subconscious content.
